Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01/03/2026

CUFitness (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the CUFitness website (the “Platform”).

By accessing or using our Platform, you agree to this Privacy Policy and our Terms and Conditions.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

  • Browser and device information
  • IP address
  • Date and time of access
  • Pages visited

1.2 Information You Provide

  • Name and email address
  • Student, coach or staff identification details
  • Membership preferences
  • Profile information
  • Payment information (processed securely by third-party providers)

2. Cookies

CUFitness uses session and persistent cookies to improve functionality and enhance user experience. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function properly.

3. Consent

By providing personal information or using the Platform, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this Policy.

4. How We Use the Information

  • Manage memberships and accounts
  • Process payments
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve platform performance and security
  • Send important service communications

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information, including encrypted data transmission and secure server environments.

6. Data Retention

Personal information is retained only as long as necessary for operational, legal, and compliance purposes. When no longer required, information is securely destroyed or anonymized.

7. Your Privacy Rights

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Withdraw consent
  • Request deletion where applicable

8. Communications

We may send emails for account updates, service notifications, and reminders for upcoming training sessions. You may unsubscribe at any time.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
